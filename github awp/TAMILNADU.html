FAMOUS FOOD IN TAMIL NADU


  1. Idli: The most famous dish in Tamilnadu, as well as the whole southern region is Idli.
  2. Sambar Best to enjoy with almost every main course, Sambar is a kind of South Indian dal (pulse).
  3. Chicken Chettinad For all non-veg lovers, one of the ideal dishes in Tamilnadu is Chicken Chettinad.
